Calculation Methodology

Surplus Value Formula

Surplus Value = (Revenue Per Employee) - (Employee Compensation)

Key Definitions

Revenue Per Employee (RPE): Total company revenue divided by number of employees.

Employee Compensation: Annualized salary + benefits (calculated as hourly wage Ã— weekly hours Ã— 52).

Data Sources

  • Industry RPE averages: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, Industry Reports
  • Private company data: S&P Capital IQ aggregates
  • Salary benchmarks: BLS Occupational Employment Statistics

Limitations & Notes

  • Values represent industry averages - individual company performance may vary.
  • For roles without direct revenue attribution (e.g., HR), we use department-level allocation.
  • Non-monetary contributions (culture, mentorship) aren't captured in this model.
